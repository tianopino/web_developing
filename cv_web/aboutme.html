About Me

Hello, I'm Gu Tian. This is me at Royal Docks, London (05/2021).I graduated with a Master's degree in Linguistics from UCL in 2021. My primary interest in linguistics are phonetics, phonology and the interdisciplinary NLP (natural language processing). I'm also interested in the various Chinese dialects, especially Wu (commonly known to the English-speaking world as Shanghainese) and Yue (Cantonese). I enjoy travelling, record collecting and learning to code during my leisure time.
